Acoino

a different type of exchange media

What is the difference?

  1. Unlike any other trading methods, acoino itself is just a trading media with zero value in it. So it cannot be traded with any form of real (or digital) currency. Otherwise, you can buy infinite amount of acoinos with $0 in theory.
  2. Acoino is of two types: acoino and antiacoino. Each of them cannot be created separately. They are always created and annihilated in pairs.
